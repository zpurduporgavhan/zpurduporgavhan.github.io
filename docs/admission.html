Admission Information

Join Z.P. Urdu School Porgavhan

📋 General Information

Z.P. Urdu School Porgavhan welcomes students from all backgrounds and communities. We provide quality education in the Urdu medium aligned with Maharashtra state curriculum.

Academic Session: June to May

School Code (UDISE): 27071105402

🎯 Eligibility Criteria

  • Students should meet the age requirements as per government standards
  • Documents: Birth Certificate, Transfer Certificate (if from another school)
  • Address proof and parent/guardian identification
  • All applicants are welcome regardless of religious or social background

📝 Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (original and copy)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous school
  • Aadhar Card or any government ID of student
  • Parent/Guardian ID proof (Aadhar/PAN/Voter ID)
  • Address proof (utility bill/rent agreement)
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
