🏫 School Overview

Z.P. Urdu School Porgavhan is an Urdu-medium primary school run under the Zilla Parishad, Amravati. The school is affiliated with Panchayat Samiti, Morshi and follows the Maharashtra state curriculum, providing quality education to children of the local community.
